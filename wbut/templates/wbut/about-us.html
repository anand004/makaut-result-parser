About Us

We at MAKAUTRESULT.in are providing a result portal for the benefit of MAKAUT Students. We are NOT in any way related to MAKAUT. This website is aimed to help the students check their results as well as their class rank.

How does this works:

Everytime you enter your roll number and semester. we fetch result from official website and store in our database for your future references.

Although we try our best, there may be errors in the information displayed in this website. So kindly verify with the official sources in case of any doubts.

We are not responsible for any loss due to such errors. You are always welcome to Contact Us for any help.
